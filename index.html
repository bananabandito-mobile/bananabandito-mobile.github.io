Privacy at a glance

Elite Member IQ is built for trust. We keep your community’s data secure and avoid collecting anything we don’t need to run the app.

Firebase-backed

We use Firebase services to authenticate users and store community data securely in the cloud.

No data sales

We do not sell your personal information. Data is only used to provide and improve the Elite Member IQ app.

Control from the app

You can manage your account and request changes to your data through the app, with a typical response time of 24 hours.
